RELX Creator Smart e-cigarettes

2025-03-01 2 min read # 电子烟 # 工业设计

Industrial Design
Designed by Zhao-X

类别 电子烟
日期 2024年
任务 市场调研，产品设计

RELX Creator专为寻求可探索和互动体验的年轻用户量身打造。其设计灵感来自积木，采用模块化设计，用户可以通过原始标准化接口将设备与不同口味和容量的烟弹搭配，从而提供丰富多样的电子烟体验。此外，通过将设备的非消耗部件与烟弹的消耗部件分离，它有效地降低了长期成本和环境影响。LED像素显示屏和个性化的表情符号反馈为用户提供了更具吸引力和愉悦感的电子烟体验。
RELX Creator is tailored for younger users seeking an explorable and interactive experience. Drawing inspiration from building blocks, it features a modular design, allowing users to pair the device with pods of varying flavors and capacities through the original standardized interface, thus facilitating a rich and diverse vaping experience. Moreover, it effectively reduces long-term costs and environmental impact by separating the device's non-consumable parts from the pod's consumable parts. The LED pixel display screen and personalized emoji feedback offer users a more engaging and enjoyable e-cigarette experience.
