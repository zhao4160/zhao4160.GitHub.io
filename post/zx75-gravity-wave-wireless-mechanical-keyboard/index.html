Zonex 75 Mechanical Keyboard

Designed by Zhao-X

类别 电脑周边

ZX75 是为“少数”发烧玩家设计的一款无线机械键盘，我们将客制化机械键盘元素和玩法带到量产机械键盘领域，让键盘爱好者以更低的门槛体验到客制化机械键盘的使用乐趣。
ZX75 is a wireless mechanical keyboard designed for "a few" passionate players. We bring customized mechanical keyboard elements and gameplay to the field of mass production mechanical keyboard, so that keyboard lovers can experience the fun of using customized mechanical keyboard with a lower threshold.
