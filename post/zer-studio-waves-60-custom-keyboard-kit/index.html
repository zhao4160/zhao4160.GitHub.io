Waves 60 Custom Keyboard kit

2025-03-01 2 min read # 电脑周边 # 键盘 # 工业设计

Industrial Design
Designed by Zhao-X

类别 电脑周边
日期 2023年
任务 市场调研，产品设计

灵感来源于“水”，这款客制化机械键盘采用简约的轮廓设计，顶部表面呈现波浪造形，模拟水波的涟漪，捕捉住时间冻结的波浪瞬间。整体美学简洁内敛，与“水”的主题完美契合，唤起一种宁静与流动感。
Waves 60 配备镜面PVD工艺的不锈钢定位板，为键盘带来如水面般平静的反射效果。结合快速回弹的泡棉垫片，这种不锈钢定位板结构为您提供了独特的打字体验和声音表现。
Inspired by "water", the keyboard features a minimalist silhouette and a wavy pattern on the top surface to mimic the ripples of water, capturing the essence of a wave frozen in time. The overall aesthetic is restrained, aligning perfectly with the theme of "Water" and evoking a sense of tranquility and fluidity.
Waves 60 features a mirror PVD finish stainless steel plate (top mirror, bottom brushed), bringing a tranquil mirror-like reflective water surface to your keyboard, With the combination of fast-rebound gaskets, the stainless steel plate structure offers you a unique typing experience and sound profile.
