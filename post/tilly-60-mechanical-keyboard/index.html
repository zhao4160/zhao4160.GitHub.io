Tilly 60 Mechanical Keyboard

The Tilly60 is a 60% layout gasket-mounted mechanical keyboard DIY kit, meticulously crafted using premium aluminum materials, ensuring exceptional durability and a superior feel. This exquisite keyboard combines sleek design with aesthetics, making it the perfect choice for those seeking a high-end experience.

Offering both HHKB and WK layout options, the compact HHKB layout ensures essential keys are easily accessible, striking a perfect balance between power and minimalism, beloved by enthusiasts of smaller form factors. Compared with HHKB, WK layout is more suitable for ordinary people to use.

The Tilly60 is meticulously built with premium aluminum, ensuring both durability and a superior feel. It features a multimedia knob for effortless volume and media control. Plus, it has a stylish ARK storage compartment for your small items, keeping your workspace neat and organized.
