M.2 硬盘盒-Stack

2024-02-02 1 min read # 电脑周边 # 工业设计

Industrial Design
Designed by Zhao-X

类别 电脑周边
日期 2019年
任务 市场调研，产品设计，包装设计

Stack 是由铝合金制成的高速 M.2 NVME 固态硬盘，散热鳍片使得产品具有更好的散热性能不需要冷却风扇。产品形状从顶部到底部逐渐变窄，波浪形为用户提供了更舒适的抓握体验。项目的灵感来自建筑，其设计理念是让用户通过设计直观的感受到产品的性能。
The Stack is a high-speed M.2 NVME SSD made of aluminum alloy, with cooling fins for better cooling performance without the need for a cooling fan. The shape of the product gradually Narrows from the top to the bottom, and the wavy shape provides a more comfortable gripping experience for the user. The project is inspired by architecture, and its design concept is to allow users to intuitively feel the performance of the product through design.
