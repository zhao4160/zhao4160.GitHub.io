双枪便携式消毒餐具盒 Eco-Dining

2024-02-03 2 min read # 餐具 # 工业设计

Industrial Design
Designed by Zhao-X

类别 餐具
日期 2016年
任务 市场调研，产品设计，包装设计

Eco-Dining 餐具盒是为在外就餐的人群设计的更便利、更健康、更环保的用餐工具。创新性的将磁吸设计应用到餐具盒，首先，用磁吸分体式开合系统有效的避免了传统的转轴、卡扣等易藏污纳垢的结构凹槽，从而提升餐具盒的易清洁性。Eco-Dining 餐具盒合上餐盒盖后， UV 消毒灯和发热片对餐具进行消毒和烘干。
Eco-Dining cutlery box is a more convenient, healthier and environmentally friendly dining tool designed for people who eat out. The innovative magnetic design is applied to the tableware box. First of all, the magnetic absorption split-type opening and closing system effectively avoids the traditional structural grooves that are easy to store dirt and dirt, such as rotating shaft and buckle, so as to improve the easy cleaning of the tableware box. After closing the lid of the Eco-Dining tableware box, the UV disinfection lamp and heating tablets disinfect and dry the tableware.
