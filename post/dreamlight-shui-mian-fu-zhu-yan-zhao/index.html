Dreamlight Zen-睡眠辅助眼罩

2024-02-04 1 min read # 穿戴 # 工业设计

Industrial Design
Designed by Zhao-X

类别 穿戴
日期 2018年
任务 市场调研，产品设计，包装设计

通过精心的设计，Dreamlight Zen 有效的为佩戴者创造了一个空间，不受到打扰无论在什么地方都可以得到充分的休息。内置橙黄色光线、多种冥想语音引导、助眠声音，同时支持蓝牙连接，满足用户多样化的使用要求。产品为可拆洗设计，取出机芯，产品外壳可以清洗，满足用户的清洁需求。
Through careful design, Dreamlight Zen effectively creates a space for the wearer to get a full rest without being disturbed, no matter where they are. Built-in orange and yellow light, a variety of meditation voice guidance, sleep aid sound, while supporting Bluetooth connection, to meet the user's diverse requirements. The product is designed for disassembly and washable, the movement is removed, and the product shell can be cleaned to meet the cleaning needs of users.
